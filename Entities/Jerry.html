Entity 7

Class: ΨA
Mental hazard
Incomprehensibly intelligent

An image of Jerry hung up on the walls of Jerry's Room. Seemingly taken in Level 30.

Do's and don'ts

Do:

  • Avoid Jerry if you’re not prepared to tame him.
  • If you plan on taming him, be sure to carry a bottle of almond water or a pack of sunflower seeds.

Don't:

  • Approach Jerry without any intention of taming him.
  • Express negative opinions about him when you're among his followers.
  • Permit yourself to fall under his indoctrination.

Discovery

One of the earliest recorded videos of the Backrooms shows a wanderer frantically fleeing from Dullers in a section of Level 0 marked with scratched writings. Despite this evidence, the origin of the wired areas where Dullers are commonly found remains a mystery. Without understanding the source of these areas, the true origin of the Dullers also remains unknown.

Description:

"Jerry" is a small bird with a parrot-like appearance that exerts mind control over anyone who holds it, compelling them to utter phrases such as "Jerry is everything," "Jerry is what I live for," and "All Hail Jerry." Shortly after falling under its influence, these individuals tend to disappear—presumably ending up in a place known as Jerry's Room, though this has not been confirmed. The bird then reappears on what seems to be a random level, ready to seek out a new host. If you encounter a bird resembling Jerry, it is advised to escape by any means possible. There is confirmed evidence that feeding Jerry sunflower seeds or almond water can tame the bird, making it safe for handling; however, this effect is limited to the individual who provided the feed, while anyone else who comes into contact with Jerry will experience its usual mind-controlling influence. It should be noted that this information is partly speculative.

Behaviors

Unless you're trying to tame him, Jerry will pursue you once he notices you. It remains under investigation whether Jerry has the ability to trigger noclipping in others. Typically, Jerry is either seen roaming Levels 1 and 2 or perched on a wooden platform in Jerry's Room, where his followers kneel and chant in his honor.

Biology

Jerry appears as a young male macaw with blue and white feathers. His primary diet consists of sunflower seeds and almond water. By carefully approaching him with either of these, you can tame him, allowing you to hold him without suffering his mind-controlling effects. After a few days of taming, Jerry will eventually take flight again, and if he encounters his former tamer, he will not attempt to assimilate them.
