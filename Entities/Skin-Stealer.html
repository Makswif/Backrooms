Entity 4

Class 4
Somewhat hazardous and unfriendly.

Behaviours:

Skin-Stealers are generally docile creatures that tend to roam aimlessly when they do not need to feed. In this passive state, they are not hostile unless provoked. However, when in a state of hunger, they become highly dangerous, actively seeking out lone humans. Once they locate their prey, they use their immense strength to tear their victims apart with their bare hands.

Discovery:

Sightings of Skin-Stealers date back many years, even before the establishment of the M.E.G. (Major Explorer Group). A database entry for these entities was created shortly after the database itself was formed, though it was updated recently to correct outdated information. As one of the most populous entities in the Backrooms, Skin-Stealers are often among the first entities that new wanderers encounter.

Description:

Entity 4, also referred to as the Skin-Stealers, are large, humanoid creatures known for using the skin of their victims as a disguise, making them challenging to recognize. These entities are often observed imitating human speech, though their attempts are notably poor. It is strongly advised to avoid any wanderer who repeats phrases or speaks in a monotone manner.

Identifying this entity was once considered impossible. However, recent discoveries have revealed several indicators that can help in recognizing it. These signs include bleeding from the ears, incoherent or jumbled speech, slow and deliberate movements, and frequent tugging at its skin. As previously mentioned, it is strongly recommended to avoid any organism exhibiting these behaviors and to flee immediately.

Skin-Stealers are among the most common entities encountered in the Backrooms, with reports of their presence spanning numerous levels. While they appear in many areas, they are most prevalent in Level 2, where they seem to thrive. Unusually, Skin-Stealers often leave their victims alive after removing significant amounts of skin. These survivors frequently succumb to blood loss shortly after the attack, but with timely intervention, some can be saved.

Do's and Don'ts:

Do:

  • If you encounter a Skin-Stealer, walk away slowly and calmly without making sudden movements.
  • Additionally, ensure you check anyone you are traveling with for signs of clear blood to confirm their humanity.

Don't:

  • Avoid trusting individuals who barely speak or utter random assortments of words, as this could indicate the presence of a Skin-Stealer.
  • Additionally, never aggravate a Skin-Stealer in its docile state, as this will provoke it into attacking.
