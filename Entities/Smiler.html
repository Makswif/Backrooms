Biology:

Smilers are characterized by their wide smile filled with sharp teeth and glowing white eyes. Their full biological makeup remains a mystery, though there are rumors of a physical body that is invisible to the human eye. Speculations suggest they may possess alien-like, non-human forms, with limbs bent in unnatural ways, but this has not been confirmed. The M.E.G. is actively investigating to uncover more about their biology.

Description:

Smilers are typically aggressive, with numerous confirmed encounters reported. They are recognizable by their distinct reflective eyes and teeth, which shine in the dark. The most effective way to evade a Smiler is to maintain eye contact. They are commonly found in dimly lit areas of Level 2 and Level 3, with their base being a location referred to as "The Smiling Room."

Do's and Don'ts:

Do:

  • Maintain eye contact and move slowly to avoid provoking them.
  • Stay silent and avoid making any loud noises.
  • Use a Smiler Exterminator bottle if available.
  • Turn off all light sources you are carrying, as light can agitate them.

Don't:

  • Run away in a panic unless the Smiler is already chasing you.
  • Make loud noises, as this can draw their attention or provoke them.
  • Keep your light sources on, as this can agitate them further.
