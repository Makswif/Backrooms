Level 0.1

Class 5
Unsafe
Unstable
Entity Infestation

Level 0.1, often known as "The Danger Zone", is a subdivision of Level 0 and bears a resemblance to the Redrooms found within that level. This area is inhabited by hostile entities, so it is wise not to remain there for too long.

An image of Level 0.1's halls

Entities:

Level 0.1 is inhabited by a diverse array of entities, including Skin-Stealers, Insanities, Hounds, Facelings, Deathmoths, and Aranea Membri. Without the presence of wanderers, Skin-Stealers tend to become significantly more aggressive and hostile toward other beings. Because wanderers seldom make it to this level, Skin-Stealers must prey on the local entities to sustain themselves.

Entrances And Exits:

Entrances

To access Level 0.1, locate a vent within Level 0 and crawl inside. Once you enter, the vent’s opening seals behind you, requiring you to continue through its entirety. The time needed to navigate the vent can vary greatly some wanderers report completing the passage in mere minutes, while others claim it has taken them days. This duration may fluctuate over time.

Additionally, be aware that any other alleged entrances to Level 0.1 not mentioned here are either unverified or outright fabrications.

Exits

Unfortunately, there have been no documented entrances on entering Level 0 from Level 0.1, instead, all exits in Level 0.1 lead you to another sub-level.

  • Finding a glass door will lead you to Level 1.1.
  • No-clipping through a glitched floor will transport you to Level -888.1.
  • If you come across a "%" symbol painted on a wall, floor, or ceiling, no-clipping into it will send you to Level 0%.
  • In The Fanmade, an invisible door can be found that teleports you to another level.

Despite the fact that there are several exits, finding them has proven to be exceedingly difficult and dangerous due to the level's abundance of hostile entities.

Description:

Although Level 0.1 shares a similar layout and design with Level 0, it exhibits several distinctive features. The overall color scheme of Level 0.1 shifts toward red and orange, as seen in its decaying wallpaper and worn-out carpeting. Uniquely, the further one ventures into this level, the more dilapidated and ruined it becomes. The once-bright red fluorescent lights gradually shatter, scattering glass shards across the carpet, while some ceiling tiles appear to have fallen out. The tinted wallpaper slowly tears, as if shredded by an unseen force. Explorers have noted that spending extended periods in Level 0.1 often leads to a mounting sense of paranoia and dread.

Colonies and Outposts

  • Even if one manages to reach this level, encountering individual human beings is extremely rare. It is estimated that there are only about 2 to 5 fully developed humans present.
  • The hostile environment can obliterate a settlement within minutes, particularly when the lights go out, and resources are almost entirely absent.
  • Establishing a colony on Level 0.1 is nearly impossible, as attempting to do so almost guarantees death. While multiple wanderers might jump into the same opening and emerge together, it remains unclear whether they could establish a lasting presence here.

Although Level 0 and Level 0.1 share an identical layout, Level 0.1 is not considered secure because Level 0 covers an area of over 600 square miles.
